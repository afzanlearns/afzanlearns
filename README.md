## Who I am

I'm a full-stack developer with a strong focus on frontend engineering, AI-powered products, and developer experience.

Most of what I build starts as a problem I personally run into. I enjoy taking ambitious ideas from concept to a polished product with an emphasis on clean interfaces, intuitive interactions, and practical automation.

---

## What I Build

Lately my work has revolved around AI developer tools, autonomous desktop agents, local-first applications, and productivity software.

I'm especially interested in creating products that reduce friction, automate repetitive work, and make complex workflows feel simple.

---

## How I Build

My primary stack revolves around **TypeScript**, **Next.js**, and **Tailwind CSS**, backed by **Node.js** whenever a backend is needed.

For AI applications, I regularly work with **Python**, local language models, retrieval systems, embeddings, and modern AI APIs to build intelligent software that runs efficiently both locally and in the cloud.

---

## Current Focus

Right now I'm building products like **Zeke** and **Perch**, while exploring autonomous systems, computer-use agents, local AI, and the future of developer tooling.
